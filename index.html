About Us

Welcome to Mama Mia !!, your number one source for all things pizza. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of pizza, with a focus on freshness, sweetness and getting you full. Founded in 2020 by Brili Kimto, Mama Mia !! has come a long way from its beginnings in a backdoor kitchen. When Brili first started out,her passion for wanting everyone to stay healthy but still eat what they wanted drove him to open this restaurant, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over Nairobi with different branches in the city. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
